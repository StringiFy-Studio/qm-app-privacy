4 Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data — including all business documents, customers, products, terms, and company information — is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. Generated PDF files are also saved to your device's internal storage.

4.2 Cloud Storage (Supabase)

Your account authentication data is stored on our cloud backend powered by Supabase — a SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure provider. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS 1.2+)
  • Encrypted cloud database storage
  • Secure authentication tokens with expiry
  • Offline-capable account sign-out for disconnected scenarios
  • No plaintext storage of passwords
