1 Introduction

Welcome to Quotation & Invoice PDF Maker ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play and the Apple App Store.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of information as described herein.

Important: QM is a document management application designed for business use. Your data - including business documents, customer information, and product details - is stored primarily on your own device. We take privacy seriously and do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Authentication method (email/password, Google, or Apple)

2.2 Business Data You Provide

The App allows you to enter and manage business-related information. This includes:

  • Company/business name, address, contact details, and tax information
  • Customer names, contact information, addresses, and tax numbers
  • Product or service names, descriptions, prices, HSN codes, and stock data
  • Quotations, invoices, and other business documents you create
  • Terms and conditions text you define
  • Bank account details and UPI ID for payment instructions in documents
  • Company logo and authorized signature images

2.3 Device and Usage Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information to operate and improve the App, including:

  • Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App version and crash or error logs
  • General usage patterns and feature interactions (analytics)
  • Internet connectivity status

2.4 Files and Media

With your permission, the App may access your device's camera and photo gallery to allow you to upload logos, signatures, and product images. Generated PDF documents are saved to your device's local storage.

4 Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data - including all business documents, customers, products, terms, and company information — is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. Generated PDF files are also saved to your device's internal storage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync features in the future.

4.2 Cloud Storage (Supabase)

Your account authentication data (email, display name, phone number, profile photo URL) is stored on our cloud backend, which is powered by Supabase - a secure, SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure provider. Profile photos are stored in Supabase's secure cloud object storage. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS. Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.

4.3 Security Measures

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational safeguards, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS 1.2+)
  • Encrypted cloud database storage
  • Secure authentication tokens with expiry
  • Offline-capable account sign-out for disconnected scenarios
  • No plaintext storage of passwords

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your device secure.

5 Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing or commercial purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party providers (such as Supabase and Firebase) solely to operate and improve the App. These providers are contractually bound to process your data only as instructed by us and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent in-app notice before your personal data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • Protection of Rights: We may share information when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of QM, our users, or the public.

You may choose to share documents (e.g., PDF quotations) with third parties using the App's share feature. We have no control over how those third parties handle information you share with them.

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the App's services and to comply with our legal obligations.

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the App or manually delete it within the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.
  • Account data (cloud): Retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your profile information from our cloud systems within 30 days, subject to any legal retention requirements.
  • Crash and analytics logs: Retained for a maximum of 90 days.

8 Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights under applicable data protection laws (such as the GDPR, CCPA, or India's DPDP Act). These rights may include:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account directly within the App (Settings → Account → Sign Out / Delete Account), or contact us.
  • Portability: Request a portable copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process data, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Object to Processing: Object to certain types of processing, including direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided in Section 11. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

Device Permissions: You can manage App permissions (camera, photo library, storage) at any time through your device's system settings.
